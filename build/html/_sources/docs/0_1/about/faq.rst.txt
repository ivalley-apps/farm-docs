Frequently Asked Questions

What's a Ivalley Farm?

  This name representing numberious cloud technologes for moden world.

How is Ivalley Farm licensed?
  It's licensed under the `Apache License v2 <http://www.apache.org/licenses/LICENSE-2.0.html>`_ .


How can I commit to Ivalley Farm?
  Go to the :grepo:`GitHub project <ivalley-apps/farm>` , fork it, and submit a pull request. We prefer small, single-purpose
  pull requests over large, multi-purpose ones. We reserve the right to turn down any proposed
  changes, but in general we're delighted when people want to make our projects better!
